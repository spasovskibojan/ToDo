What is a ToDo list?

A ToDo list, also known as a task list or a to-do list, is a tool or a simple organizational technique used to manage tasks, activities, or items that need to be accomplished. It is a way to keep track of what needs to be done and helps individuals or teams stay organized and focused on their goals.

The main purpose of a ToDo list is to provide a centralized location to record tasks and prioritize them based on importance, deadlines, or other criteria. It helps individuals remember what needs to be done, stay organized, and maintain productivity by providing a clear overview of their responsibilities. As tasks are completed, they can be checked off or marked as completed, providing a sense of accomplishment and progress.

ToDo lists can be used on an individual basis or within a team or organization to coordinate and manage tasks collectively. They are a popular productivity tool and can be customized based on personal preferences and specific requirements.
